Aloe Vera (Aloe barbadensis miller)

A versatile medicinal plant known for its healing properties and skin benefits.

Scientific Information

Family: Asphodelaceae

Native Region: Arabian Peninsula

Plant Type: Succulent perennial

Height: 60-100 cm

Flowering Season: Summer

Traditional Significance

Aloe Vera has been valued across cultures:

  • Ancient Egyptian healing plant
  • Traditional skin treatment
  • Ayurvedic medicine component
  • Natural beauty aid
  • Digestive health support

Medicinal Properties

  • Anti-inflammatory action
  • Wound healing properties
  • Antioxidant benefits
  • Immune system support
  • Digestive aid
  • Skin moisturizing

Common Uses

  • Skin care products
  • Wound treatment
  • Digestive health
  • Hair care
  • Natural moisturizer
  • Sunburn relief

Cultivation Tips

  • Well-draining soil
  • Bright indirect light
  • Minimal watering
  • Protect from frost
  • Regular pruning
  • Container gardening suitable

Safety Precautions

  • Use fresh gel carefully
  • Avoid during pregnancy
  • Monitor blood sugar
  • Patch test for skin use
  • Consult healthcare provider
  • Use in moderation
