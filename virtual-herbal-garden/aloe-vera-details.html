Aloe Vera

Scientific Information

Scientific Name: Aloe barbadensis miller

Common Names: Aloe Vera, Ghritkumari, Kumari

Family: Asphodelaceae

Origin: Arabian Peninsula, now cultivated worldwide in tropical and subtropical regions.

Traditional Significance

Aloe Vera has been used for thousands of years in traditional medicine across various cultures, including Ayurveda, where it's known as Ghritkumari.

Medicinal Properties

  • Skin healing and moisturizing
  • Digestive health support
  • Anti-inflammatory properties
  • Wound healing
  • Immune system support

Common Uses

  • Topical application for burns and skin conditions
  • Internal consumption for digestive health
  • Natural moisturizer in cosmetics
  • Traditional remedy for various ailments
