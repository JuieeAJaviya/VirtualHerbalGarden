Ashwagandha

Scientific Information

Scientific Name: Withania somnifera

Common Names: Ashwagandha, Indian Ginseng, Winter Cherry

Family: Solanaceae

Origin: Native to India, North Africa, and the Middle East.

Traditional Significance

Ashwagandha is one of the most important herbs in Ayurvedic medicine, known as a powerful adaptogen and rejuvenating herb.

Medicinal Properties

  • Adaptogenic properties (stress management)
  • Energy enhancement
  • Immune system support
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • Sleep improvement
  • Cognitive function enhancement

Common Uses

  • Stress and anxiety management
  • Energy and vitality enhancement
  • Sleep quality improvement
  • Physical performance enhancement
  • Traditional tonic for overall health
