Brahmi

Scientific Information

Scientific Name: Bacopa monnieri

Common Names: Brahmi, Water Hyssop, Thyme-leaved Gratiola

Family: Plantaginaceae

Origin: Native to India, found in wetlands and marshy areas throughout the Indian subcontinent.

Traditional Significance

Brahmi is one of the most revered herbs in Ayurvedic medicine, known for its powerful effects on brain function and cognitive abilities. It's named after Brahma, the creator in Hindu mythology.

Medicinal Properties

  • Memory enhancement
  • Brain function improvement
  • Anti-anxiety effects
  • Antioxidant properties
  • Neuroprotective effects
  • Stress reduction

Common Uses

  • Memory and learning enhancement
  • Stress and anxiety management
  • Traditional brain tonic
  • Mental clarity improvement
  • Ayurvedic medicine for cognitive health
