Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri)

A sacred cognitive enhancer known as the "Herb of Grace" renowned for its memory-boosting and mental clarity properties.

Scientific Information

Family: Plantaginaceae

Native Region: Indian Subcontinent

Plant Type: Perennial creeper

Height: 10-30 cm

Flowering Season: Year-round

Traditional Significance

Brahmi holds special importance in Ayurveda:

  • Named after Lord Brahma
  • Sacred herb in Ayurveda
  • Used in ancient texts
  • Memory enhancement herb
  • Symbol of knowledge

Medicinal Properties

  • Cognitive enhancement
  • Memory improvement
  • Anti-anxiety effects
  • Neuroprotective properties
  • Antioxidant action
  • Mental clarity support

Common Uses

  • Memory enhancement
  • Mental focus
  • Stress reduction
  • Brain tonic
  • Hair and skin care
  • Anxiety management

Cultivation Tips

  • Moist, well-draining soil
  • Partial to full sun
  • Regular watering
  • Space plants 20-30 cm apart
  • Warm climate preferred
  • Can grow in water

Safety Precautions

  • Start with small doses
  • May cause digestive issues
  • Avoid during pregnancy
  • Can interact with medications
  • Monitor blood pressure
  • Consult healthcare provider
