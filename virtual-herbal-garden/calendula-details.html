Calendula

Scientific Information

Scientific Name: Calendula officinalis

Common Names: Calendula, Marigold, Pot Marigold

Family: Asteraceae

Origin: Native to the Mediterranean region, now cultivated worldwide.

Traditional Significance

Calendula has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for its healing properties, particularly for skin conditions and wound healing.

Medicinal Properties

  • Skin healing
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • Antimicrobial properties
  • Antioxidant activity
  • Wound healing
  • Immune system support

Common Uses

  • Skin care and wound treatment
  • Inflammation reduction
  • Traditional medicine
  • Cosmetic applications
  • Natural dye
  • Ornamental plant
