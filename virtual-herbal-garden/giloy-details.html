Giloy

Scientific Information

Scientific Name: Tinospora cordifolia

Common Names: Giloy, Guduchi, Amrita

Family: Menispermaceae

Origin: Native to the Indian subcontinent, found throughout tropical and subtropical regions.

Traditional Significance

Giloy is considered one of the most important herbs in Ayurvedic medicine. Its Sanskrit name "Amrita" means "immortality," reflecting its powerful healing properties.

Medicinal Properties

  • Immune system enhancement
  • Fever reduction
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • Antioxidant properties
  • Liver protection
  • Blood sugar regulation

Common Uses

  • Fever management
  • Immune system strengthening
  • Digestive health improvement
  • Traditional medicine for various ailments
  • Ayurvedic tonic for overall health
