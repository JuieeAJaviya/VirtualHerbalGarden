Lavender (Lavandula angustifolia)

A calming herb known for its soothing aroma and therapeutic properties.

Scientific Information

Family: Lamiaceae

Native Region: Mediterranean

Plant Type: Perennial herb

Height: 30-60 cm

Flowering Season: Summer

Traditional Significance

Lavender has been valued across cultures:

  • Ancient calming herb
  • Traditional sleep aid
  • Medicinal wisdom herb
  • Cultural significance
  • Natural fragrance

Medicinal Properties

  • Calming effects
  • Sleep support
  • Anti-inflammatory action
  • Antimicrobial effects
  • Pain relief
  • Stress reduction

Common Uses

  • Aromatherapy
  • Herbal tea
  • Essential oil
  • Sleep aid
  • Natural fragrance
  • Skin care

Cultivation Tips

  • Well-draining soil
  • Full sunlight
  • Minimal watering
  • Regular pruning
  • Protect from frost
  • Container suitable

Safety Precautions

  • Use in moderation
  • Avoid during pregnancy
  • Monitor for allergies
  • Watch for interactions
  • Consult healthcare provider
  • Use essential oil carefully
