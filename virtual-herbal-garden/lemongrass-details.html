Lemongrass

Scientific Information

Scientific Name: Cymbopogon citratus

Common Names: Lemongrass, Citronella Grass, Fever Grass

Family: Poaceae

Origin: Native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Sri Lanka.

Traditional Significance

Lemongrass has been used in traditional medicine across Asia for centuries, particularly in Ayurveda and traditional Chinese medicine.

Medicinal Properties

  • Digestive health support
  • Anti-anxiety effects
  • Antimicrobial properties
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • Fever reduction
  • Pain relief

Common Uses

  • Digestive issues treatment
  • Stress and anxiety management
  • Fever reduction
  • Natural insect repellent
  • Culinary uses
  • Aromatherapy
