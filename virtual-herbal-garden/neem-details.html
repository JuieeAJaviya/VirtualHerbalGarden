Neem

Scientific Information

Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica

Common Names: Neem, Indian Lilac, Margosa

Family: Meliaceae

Origin: Native to the Indian subcontinent, now cultivated in tropical and semi-tropical regions worldwide.

Traditional Significance

Neem is considered a sacred tree in Indian culture and has been used in traditional medicine for thousands of years. It's often called the "village pharmacy" due to its wide range of medicinal uses.

Medicinal Properties

  • Antibacterial and antifungal properties
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • Blood purifying properties
  • Immune system enhancement
  • Skin health benefits
  • Dental health support

Common Uses

  • Skin care and treatment of skin conditions
  • Dental hygiene (neem twigs for brushing)
  • Natural pesticide and insect repellent
  • Traditional medicine for various ailments
  • Environmental purification
