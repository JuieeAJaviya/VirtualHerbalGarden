Pudina (Mint)

Scientific Information

Scientific Name: Mentha spicata

Common Names: Pudina, Spearmint, Garden Mint

Family: Lamiaceae

Origin: Native to Europe and Asia, now cultivated worldwide.

Traditional Significance

Mint has been used in traditional medicine for thousands of years, particularly in Ayurveda and traditional Chinese medicine, for its cooling and digestive properties.

Medicinal Properties

  • Digestive aid
  • Cooling properties
  • Anti-inflammatory effects
  • Antimicrobial properties
  • Respiratory health support
  • Nausea relief

Common Uses

  • Digestive health improvement
  • Natural breath freshener
  • Culinary uses
  • Traditional medicine
  • Aromatherapy
  • Natural cooling agent
