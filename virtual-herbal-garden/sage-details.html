Sage (Salvia officinalis)

A revered herb known for its memory-enhancing properties and culinary value.

Scientific Information

Family: Lamiaceae

Native Region: Mediterranean

Plant Type: Perennial herb

Height: 30-70 cm

Flowering Season: Early summer

Traditional Significance

Sage has been valued across cultures:

  • Ancient Roman sacred herb
  • Traditional memory enhancer
  • Medicinal wisdom symbol
  • Cultural culinary herb
  • Spiritual cleansing herb

Medicinal Properties

  • Memory enhancement
  • Anti-inflammatory action
  • Antioxidant benefits
  • Antimicrobial effects
  • Digestive support
  • Hormonal balance

Common Uses

  • Memory improvement
  • Culinary seasoning
  • Herbal tea
  • Natural preservative
  • Aromatherapy
  • Traditional medicine

Cultivation Tips

  • Well-draining soil
  • Full sunlight
  • Moderate watering
  • Regular pruning
  • Protect from frost
  • Container suitable

Safety Precautions

  • Use in moderation
  • Avoid during pregnancy
  • Monitor blood pressure
  • Watch for interactions
  • Consult healthcare provider
  • Use fresh leaves carefully
